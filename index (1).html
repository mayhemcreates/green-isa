How does a Green ISA work?

Every penny in your Green ISA will be invested in a company that is Green certified.

This means that you can be confident your money is helping Green companies thrive, grow and make a positive impact in the world

You will be kept regularly updated on what these companies are doing and how they're having an impact. This will be completely transparent and help you understand how we're changing the world together.
